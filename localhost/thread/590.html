Recently, I've noticed an obsession with people stealing Discord tokens, like Paul_GD a few months ago and CrankySuperToon from the evlUtion discord server. I also like seeing how the sonic fanbase truely is. Remember Sonic Gather Battle? Yeah, this is another sonic fangame that kind of a virus.



We currently don't know the exact reason for this yet, but hopefully it isn't another OC problem.
The exact effects, however, are known: he'll steal your discord Token. Now, you may ask, what is a token? Well, in an easy way, lets just say it gives other people access to your account. It's like giving away your password, or leaving the keys to your home.
If you already ran this, change your password **immediately**. If you want extra security, enable 2FA on your discord account. Hopefully it isn't too late.

Credits to the Sonic Mania Mods Discord Server, for telling us about this.

-Do not accept friend requests from random people Sorry maor, I had to do this.
