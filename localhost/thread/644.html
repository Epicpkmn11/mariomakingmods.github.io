What is Retro Revival?
Retro Revival is a project that aims to bring back everything SMB and other games had that SMM doesn't. This project will include:
  • Old Graphics (such as Lakitu cloud)
  • Costume(s)
  • Themes
And more to come!
Of course, I don't plan on doing this alone. Anyone with good experience in making graphics for Mario Maker can comment below if you would like to join the team. A beta release will hopefully come within the next 2 months.
(Note, this may or may not be a major project. It will depend on how much content is in the final product.)
UPDATE: Beta 1 is now out! Download below.
https://www.dropbox.com/s/0kzjpc9vm45nzil/project-smb_beta-1.zip?dl=0 - Beta 1
