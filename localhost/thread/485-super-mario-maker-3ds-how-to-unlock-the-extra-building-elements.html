Hey, hey, hey guys. Today i will explain how to unlock several parts you can use for your own levels. But obviously, to get them, you will have to do something in return. What is that thing you need to do? To unlock everything, you need to complete the Super Mario Mission feature once and once you do it again, at 12-3 you'll receive secret levels. But i'll explain it all in here. :)


You have several of elements you already have, like a Warp Pipe, the Brick Blocks, the ?-Blocks, etc.. For short, elements you could use for one single overworld level, so it's not exactly what you call "a good amount of choices". And that's where Super Mario Mission is for... Well, sort of. You also can use Super Mario Mission to get away from boredom. But Super Mario Mission is also useful to unlock these things in these worlds:

The 12 secret levels that will appear in Levelbot can be unlocked at 40 medals and completing Super Mario Mission. And here is what you get once you complete the 12 secret levels:

Pink Coins(*10) - Clear the 12 secret levels
Bowser Jr. - Clear the 12 secret levels

Alright, that's all for now. If i missed some info, i would love to update this guide. For now, i will say: Have fun with building levels! And do not forget, the guides the pigeon and the woman in the game have are useful! Just follow all lessons and you'll know why!


Meanings of the (*) number symbols:

(*1) = A Rocky Wrench is a mole that throws Wrenches on airships. They can be beaten by jumping on them when going out of their hiding spot, which is on the floor.
(*2) = A Lava Bubble isn't actually a bubble at all. It's a ball of fire that jumps out of lava non-stop. The higher you place it in the game, the higher it reaches.
(*3) = A Spike Top is like a Buzzy Beetle, but a Spike Top can walk on walls and ceilings and has one spike on its shell, making it immune for jumping and fire balls. Stars can beat them, though.
(*4) = Boo Buddies are a circle of a bunch of boos, and a gap is in the circle too. They float around the spot where you hold them and they are like normal Boos.
(*5) = A Muncher is a plant that is known as the hardest enemy in the Mario franchise. Why? Because only a POW-Block defeats them. And nothing else!
(*6) = A Koopa Clown Car is originally Bowser's vehicle, and can be used by anything to fly. Enemies and even items use it or the fire variant to attack Mario, and the player can use it to fly/attack with Y/X (hold to charge a bigger fireball)
(*7) = A Bumper is the oppositie of the Grinder (= a saw of 3x3 blocks), because a Bumper bumps Mario away. Only enemies and items just go through them, though. It's useful for a machine where Mario can't have access to!
(*8) = A Skewer is an invincible pillar with spikes. They hide somewhere, then pull back and extend to crush Mario. Enemies and items don't have any trouble with these things.
(*9) = Magikoopa is a Koopa with a magic wand and blue sorcerer clothes. They fire magic shapes at Mario and once it hits a block, it turns into an enemy or an item (yes, 1-UP Mushrooms included!) Then they teleport to somewhere else at the scene.
(*10) = Pink Coins are used to make a key. And the maximum pink coins in one level is 10. Just figure out yourself HOW you get them. :P But here's a hint: In the main area, only 5 coins can fit.
