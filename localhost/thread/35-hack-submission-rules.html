Here are some basic rules and guidelines to help keep your hacks looking nice and the forum easy to navigate.



1. Mark your thread with the following tags:
  • Ongoing- Your hack is currently being worked on. Any hack that hasn't been updated by a team member withing 3 months using this tag will be trashed.
  • Released- Your hack is finished and has been published. A download link must be clearly shown on your hack thread.
  • Hiatus- Your hack is not currently being worked on. A hack can stay on Hiatus for up to 12 months before it's trashed.
  • Dead- Your hack is no longer being worked on by anyone. It will then either be moved to our archive or the trash.



2. Your thread MUST include:
  • A good description of the hack.
  • AT LEAST 3 Good quality Screenshots. (videos of levels are fine as well)
  • It MUST have at least 1 COMPLETED WORLD IN THE PATCH FILES.
  • The hack should meet a minimum quality criteria:
    • Good level design. Levels should be enjoyable and not trollish types of levels.
    • Completely original levels. Levels ripped from other games with re-skins will not be accepted
    • Absolutely no glitched or broken graphics or tiles.

    Threads that do not comply with these rules will be trashed within 24 hours.
