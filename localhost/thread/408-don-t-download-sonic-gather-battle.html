A Sonic fangame by the name of "Sonic Gather Battle" is not safe to download.
It has a malicious DRM that actives when the player tries to hack/cheat the fangame.
This malicious DRM could give the creator of this fangame access to information on your computer.
Things like your passwords, credit card information, etc.

Unfortunately, if you have downloaded and played this fangame, the creator has your IP and possibly more of your information.

I advise everyone to not download this fangame or else some of your information could be in the hands of others.
