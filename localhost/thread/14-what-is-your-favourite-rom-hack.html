 		Good thread to start off the community discussion in this forum and on something partly relevant.

What is/are your favourite ROM hack(s)? My top 2 are:

* Super Mario Bros. Next: An older SMB1 hack with extremely good level design. Pretty hard to find because there's an NSMB2 mod of the same name, it seems, and the fact it's quite an old hack. Longplay Video

* Super Mario Kart - Pro Edition: A good Super Mario Kart hack, forgot the author's name. It's also quite an old hack. Video of the Special Cup

* Sonic 2: The Lost Worlds - A Sonic 2 hack that more accurately restores the lost levels of Sonic 2, including the infamous Genocide City Zone as (mostly) how it should have been. Longplay Video

Yeah, I didn't say Newer, or anything modern, because I only play classic games and (currently) hacks I have found in my ROM collections, so haven't played the newer hacks and such.
