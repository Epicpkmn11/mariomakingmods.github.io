 		Surprisingly, I made a lot of puzzle courses back in the day.
Luckily, they aren't hard at all!
No, really. They're pretty simple.

These two courses are 2 quick puzzles. They don't take long to
do, but you'll need to figure it out first. That's the challenge.

In this puzzle, you are't allowed to jump, unless you want to die.
You have to use anything in the area to support you.

This puzzle could be improved, since you can jump in some parts.



7BAC-0000-0291-AF91
This is a unique one. The challenge isn't figuring out what to do.
It's actually executing it.

It involves timing. If you're good at that, you should be fine. :)



D0EB-0000-02AE-285B

These are some other puzzle courses.

This puzzle uses an idea and expands on it. Using a shelmet,
you can bump up enemies. This mechanic was utilized to create
this course! However, this is a little lengthy.



DD74-0000-0243-F38C
Okay, ignore the title; this is a piece of cake. I literally made
this in 10 minutes. It is not hard at all. Some sections aren't
even puzzles. They are just mechanics that everyone knows about.

Yes, Mayro, even you can do this.



7031-0000-0221-08D5

Finally, these are my favorite puzzle courses. You could say I saved the best for last.

As the title says, this level uses small ideas and puts it together in one course.
There are Headbutt puzzles, aim challenges, note block gimmicks, and more!

This is a very fun course. You should enjoy it! :D



8924-0000-0291-A08D
This is an interesting castle. I was making a course that had whatever
I wanted in it, but then, I incorporated Headbutt puzzles, Bullet Launcher
gimmicks, and a boss fight at the end. It's a nice course with cool mechanics
inside of it. You should like it. :)

Oh. It also looks pretty. There's that, I guess.



CB81-0000-0291-8BF5
