Since I might make more colors in the future the download only includes the SZS files and not the entire StaticSkin.pack.
But here is a quick tutorial on how to put those files in the StaticSkin.pack of the game:
First you're going to need Wexo's Toolbox (download here http://wiki.tockdom.com/wiki/Wexos%27s_Toolbox)
Open Wexo's Toolbox and go to File­­>Open



Search for your StaticSkin.pack file (you can get one directly from the game or from mods on this site that feature one)



Open it and find "WU_Player_Yoshi.szs" (wich is easier said than done). Then right click and select "Replace".



Replace it with the "WU_Player_Yoshi" file of this mod.



Repeat this step with "WU_Ennemy_kutsu_kuriboA.szs".



And with "WU_Ennemy_kutsu_kuriboB.szs".



Once you are done go to File and click "Save".



You can now close Wexo's Toolbox, put the StaticSkin.pack on your SD card and enjoy your shiny new black Yoshi.
