Super Mario Maker: Vanilla Revamped is a Super Mario Maker mod that aims to enhance
Super Mario Maker's graphics, audio, and more to improve upon the original game.






Super Mario Maker: Vanilla Revamped Beta 1.4.1 has only modified SMB1.

Latest Version: 1.4.1






Nintendo 3DS & Nintendo Switch versions are not available currently.
