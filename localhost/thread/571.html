So here we are, 2.5 years after Super Mario Maker for Wii U was announced. Lots of stuff has changed since its release, like all the new features, new updates and it even got a 3DS port. However, all of that went to waste, when Nintendo announced that the Wii U is dead, and Miiverse was shut down. and with the recent activity that both [NSMBW hacking](https://horizonwii.net/) and [NSMBU hacking](https://discord.gg/3FZKuDv) are getting alot more active, I've wondered who here actually hacks it.

For me, I hack it alot more than actually play it. When I do play it, I like to make levels that are more fun, casual and creative oriented than actual serious levels. I might make a new level soon, but I still do have the contest levels to play. Also, um, I do still hack it. In fact, I have 2 unreleased themes to release.
