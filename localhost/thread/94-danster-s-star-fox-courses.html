 		I made a lot of Star Fox courses for some reason. Some long, some short.
These first three are the ones that take place in a box.

This is the first "box" course I ever made. I called it "Star Fox Rip-Off"
as an inside joke, and a lack for a better name. Keep in mind that this
is an old course, as it came out in September, when the game first launched.

There's a little Bowser encounter inside the course to represent
that head guy from the Star Fox games.



65BC-0000-0097-4123

From the success of the first course, I decided to make a sequel.
The box now resembles a rocket ship, and it actually looks like you're
in space. There are some different enemies coming toward you this time.

The ending has you go up against Bowser who represents
that head guy from the Star Fox games. Then, there
is a twist/surprise ending.



FF2B-0000-009D-75E9

Once, I was looking at my old courses. Then I came to
a realization; the first "Star Fox Rip-Off" course was
ugly! So I decided to remake and add to it.

This course is now in the SMW game style. Also, Yoshi
and other powerups appear in the ship's new storage
spot. Other than that, the course is basically the first
one, but better. So of course, Bowser will still be there
to represent that head guy from the Star Fox games.

You know. Andross!



8D52-0000-020E-5C88

Those are all the "box" levels. Let's move on to the one and only Shoot'em up level.

I hadn't made a Shmup level before. So I thought it was
about time. The course is a Shmup, but it's also autoscrolling.

Just thought I'd let you guys on that little secret. :P



AA4B-0000-019B-1541

The last course is a short and sweet course for practicing. :3

No story for this one. It just kinda... happened. :P

In this course, you use statonary Fire Clown Cars and
you aim at a question block on the other side of the room.
There is a key inside it, and it can only be obtained by
destroying the block. However, there are bullet launchers
that will block your fire. So you need to time your shots.

This is a quick and easy one. Shouldn't take too long. :P
