Sonic has become his smaller self with this Mystery Mushroom mod! This is a mod I made a while ago, then forgot about, but I remembered and posted it! This mod, of course, goes over Sonic the Hedgehog. You can choose between Sonic 1/CD colours and Sonic Mania colours.

The sprites for this mod were made by Mr. Porkchop (https://www.spriters-resource.com/submitter/Mr.+Porkchop/) on The Spriter's Resource, and were imported by me. The sprites were imported using both Wexos's Toolbox and Paint.NET.
