Hi guys! Today I'm coming to you with a tutorial. This means a lot to the Mario maker modding community, so be sure to try it!
WARNING: Do not follow this tutorial unless you KNOW WHAT YOU ARE DOING. You have been warned.
Requirements:
Homebrew Channel
Mocha CFW
FTPiiU Everywhere
(Optional) WiiU Title Boot Editor - https://gamebanana.com/tools/6397
Your preferred FTP software, Filezilla, etc
Mario Maker on your hard drive or USB
___
Step 1: On your Wiiu, open the Homebrew Channel and boot up Mocha CFW.
Step 2: Launch Homebrew Channel again, and open FTPiiU Everywhere. Write down the IP address it shows you.
Step 3: On your computer, boot up your preferred FTP software, and log in. If done correctly, a bunch of code will appear on your wiiu screen. Keep it on.
Step 4: Depending on where you live or where Mario Maker is stored on your wiiu, it might be different. Follow the steps below:
Stored on Hard Drive > storage_mlc > usr > title
Stored on USB > storage_usb > usr > title
Step 5: title > 0050000 > ?
? = titleID of Mario Maker. This will differ depending on where you live.
Step 6: Now you're good to go! Just put the mods in the corresponding folder, which you can get from mariomods.net.
NOTE: DO NOT TOUCH THE META FOLDER. You will learn how to edit this in the rest of this tutorial.
Also, I'd recommend to keep a backup of all your files. Just know that you could be banned when uploading levels with mods, modded thumbnail, etc.
(THE REST IS OPTIONAL)
Step 7: Keep FTPiiU Evaerywhere open, but close your software and open Wiiu TItle Boot Editor.
Step 8: Connect to your wiiu
Step 9: Edit the files! This is a thread expecting for you to know how to find the files, so if you need help, just comment below.
So yeah! I hope you guys enjoyed this tutorial! See ya soon!
