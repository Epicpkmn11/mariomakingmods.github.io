 		Well first of all you need to make sure that your arc file is compressed as LH, use ntcompress to do so: http://www.mediafire.com/file/kdc94w0rs9dx72y/ntcompress.exe (put those files in a folder as well as your level and double click on the "compress" file). To put it in your iso select the file that ends with ARC.LH, hit "replace" and replace it with the ".bin" version of your level. Also what editor are you using, Reggie or ReggieNext ?
