Hello! It's my first hack. Well, New Super Mario Bros. Modern is full NSMB hack which features:

  • New graphics - rips from NSMBW/U and Newer DS
  • New sound effects like coin collect, power-up animation etc.
  • Two new tracks in sound_data.sdat - BGM_MORI for jungle and BGM_SETSUGEN for snow
  • Some physics improvements

Screenshots:




Current progress:
- World 1 - 1-1, 1-2, 1-3, 1-4, 1-5
- World 2 - 2-1, 2-4, 2-6
- World 3 - nothing
- World 4 - 4-1, 4-2, 4-4, 4-A
- World 6 - pretty half featuring mountain theme


Special thanks and credits to:
  • TRS for bringing NSMBW/2/U rips to NSMBHD
  • MeroMero for ASM hacks, jyotyu and SubNohara tilesets
  • Newer DS Team, including RicBent and Skawo for NSMBU pipes, Pipe Joints, Goombas and Koopa Troopas
  • RoadrunnerWMC, Hiccup and Treeki for NSMBU underground and blue sky tileset
  • NSMBe Team

ASM hacks:
  • Star Coins are worth 4000 points
  • Double SubNohara size (including snowy castles)
