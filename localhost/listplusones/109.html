 		So recently..
Louiskovski made a SMAS mod for SMM.
They released it yesterday, and it turns out..
they figured out how to add backgrounds!

I downloaded the mod, and I figured out how they did it.
I'll be teaching you how to make new backgrounds!
This is the way I do it at least..

Programs Needed:

Wexos's Toolbox
Hex Editor
yamlconv
Notepad++

Step 1. Copy any background file from your SMM dump. (M1_DV_Plain.szs, etc.)


Step 2. Paste it into another folder.


Step 3. Open the szs file in Wexos's Toolbox.


Step 4. Right click "Output.bfres" and click export. Export into the folder with your szs file.


Step 5. Open the bfres file in your favorite hex editor.


Step 6. Press CTRL + F and search for the first two characters of your szs file. In this case, it's "M1"


Step 7. Replace every "M1" with something else, like "MA", "MB", "M4", "M5", etc. (Do not name the file M1, M3, MW, or WU.)


Step 8. Press CTRL + S to save, and then close the hex editor.

Step 9. Do your modifications to the BFRES file for the new background.

Step 10. Right click Output.bfres in Wexos's Toolbox, and click "Replace".


Step 11. Navigate to your BFRES file and click "Open".

Step 12. Save the SZS file.


Step 13. Rename the SZS file to match with the previous change. (In this case, It's MA_DV_Plain.szs)

Step 14. Copy a file called "Static.pack" from your SMM dump to the folder.


Step 15. Open it in Wexos's Toolbox and search for a file called "XX_SceneDB.byaml" (XX replaced with M1, M3, MW, and WU)


Step 16. Right click it and export it to the folder.


Step 17. Download "yamlconv" and put it into the folder.


Step 18. Drag "XX_SceneDB.byaml" onto yamlconv.exe to convert it to an XML file.


Step 19. Open the XML file in Notepad++
It should look similar to this.


Step 20. Let's say I want to change the underground background.


Step 21. Where it says <DV type="string">?????</DV>, replace ?????? with the name of your szs file. (????? can be M0_DV_black, MW_DV_plain, etc.)



Step 22. Save the XML file.

Step 23. Delete the BYAML file in the folder.

Step 24. Drag the XML file onto yamlconv.exe to convert it to BYAML.

Step 25. Open Static.pack in Wexos's Toolbox, and search for "XX_SceneDB.byaml" again.

Step 26. Right click it, and click "Replace". Navigate to the new BYAML, click it, and click "Open."

Step 27. Press CTRL + S to save Static.pack

Step 28. Delete the BFRES, BYAML and XML file.

Step 29. Copy "XX_DV_Plain.SZS" and "Static.pack" to your SD card.

Step 30. Run the new files on your Wii U with SDCafiine 1.4

Woah! 30 steps to just add a background. I hope this tutorial was useful for you,
and I hope it helps you make some amazing mods in the future!

Credits to GRAnimated for helping me with the hex editing bit.

 		How to play Super Mario Maker for Nintendo 3DS mods using Luma3DS!

Step 1: Make sure you have Luma3DS installed! ( This guide should be helpful: https://3ds.hacks.guide/ )

Step 2: Remove the SD Card from your 3DS system and put it in your PC.

Step 3: Place your mod files in <sdcardname>\luma\titles\<titleidhere>\romfs

Title IDs

JP - 00040000001A0300

US - 00040000001A0400

EU - 00040000001A0500

It should look something like this. (Maybe not exactly but at least similar.)


Step 4: Eject your SD Card from your PC and put it in your 3DS system.

Step 5: Make sure you have game patching enabled, then press START to save. ( To check, hold select upon booting your 3DS system. )

Step 6: Open Super Mario Maker for Nintendo 3DS.

Step 7: You should be finished! Congratulations.

If there are any errors, please let me know.

(Hidden Step 8: To play normal SMM3DS, turn off game patching.)
