Welcome to Mario Making Mods

We are a community dedicated to bringing you a home for the hacking of Super Mario Maker (Wii U & 3DS).
Our community is a place for everyone to post their projects, get help and support, and find tutorials.
Join us to share your very own creations, or feel free to just browse the site and check out some of the awesome projects and tutorials found here.

Super Mario Maker Mods









We have all sorts of crazy mods for Super Mario Maker, ranging from Super Mario Odyssey in 8-bit style, to Super Mario Maker: Vanilla Revamped.
Whatever you are interested in, you might find it in our Depot.
Over at the Depot, we have links to download the best mods out there for Super Mario Maker & tutorials for newcomers.

Wiki

At the Mario Making Mods Wiki, you'll find lots of up-to-date information about all the technical parts of Super Mario Maker. You can submit your own findings there, too!

WiiU 5.5.2 FW release

Posted on 07-24-17, 02:39 pm by NightYoshi370
Yes, I know that I'm late, but better late than never.

Nintendo’s older console just received a new update. All system owners can download version 5.5.2.

The update notes haven’t been exciting for Wii U in awhile, and nothing really changed this time either. Nintendo simply states, “Further improvements to overall system stability and other minor adjustments have been made to enhance the user experience”.

What this actually patches is the WebBrowser exploits, so you'll have to rely on Haxchi.

This also hints at Miiverse shutting down. While we don't know when its going to shut down, we also don't know if it will affect SMM in any way, shape or form, so I would recommend using Loadliine to rip your courses to your PC so that you can upload it to the SMMDB, and Saveliine so you can transfer downloaded courses onto your SMM save.
