Hello! This is a theme that I created based off Castle Bleck from Super Paper Mario. I chose to make it replace a SMB theme because the SPM jump abilities were quite limited and you can play as Luigi, Peach, and Bowser in SMB using mystery mushrooms. I should point out that I got some of the graphics from SuperArthurBros but the rest of these totally complex tiles were made by yours truly.
