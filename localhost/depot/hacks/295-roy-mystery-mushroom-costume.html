My second released Super Mario Maker mod!

Our Boy, the Fire Emblem warrior, Roy has joined Super Mario Maker as an Amiibo costume!

Going over Lucina, he has fitting sound effects and is next to the other Fire Emblem characters!

Special thanks to Kirby-Kid on Deviantart (https://kirby-kid.deviantart.com/) for making the sprites, as well as one or two more I plan on making in the future!

If any bugs are found with this mod, let me know and I'll fix them!
