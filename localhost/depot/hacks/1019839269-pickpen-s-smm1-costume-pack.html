This is just a little fun mod I wanted to do that replaces some characters with new ones into Super Mario Maker 1.

Characters features in this mod:
- Link from Super Mario Maker 2 is now in SMM1
- Klonoa replaces the Palutena costume
- Doraemon replaces the Pit costume
- Tails replaces the Sonic costume

Sprites ripped by Frario, AzRaezel, Superjustinbros, Random Talking Bush
