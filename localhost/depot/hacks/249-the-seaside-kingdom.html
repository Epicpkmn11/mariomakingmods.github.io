Here's Bubblaine!

Replacing SMB1's Overworld theme is Super Mario Odyssey's Seaside Kingdom! Here you can find sandy ground, purple pipes, and umbrellas to boot. There is no custom music for when editing a level, but the overworld makes up for it with amazing music from Tater-Tot Tunes! (Seriously, check em' out, they're amazing!)
