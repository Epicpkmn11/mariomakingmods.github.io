Posted on 12-18-17, 02:29 pm by Lakifume blarg
Hey everyone, I'm back with a new mod and this time for SMB3. If you are like me and loved the original game on the NES then this mod is for you. It replaces the airship with the tank theme from world 8.

There is a little preview of what it looks like:

I used sideway pipes as the floor and brick blocks as burners extensions.

Features:
-tileset adapted to the theme
-recolored background
-new conveyor belts
-new burners
-invisible tracks (because why not)

Issues:
-the airship rocking is still there (wich looks a little weird in a tank level)
-the custom conveyors also affect the overworld theme

Download Wii U:
http://www.mediafire.com/file/c0sa48lvs8qw2n8/Tank.rar

Put those two folders in the content folder of the game. Have fun!

-MasterVermillion for helping me fix a burner glitch I had
