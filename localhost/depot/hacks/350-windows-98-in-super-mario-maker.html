Have you ever thought, "Man it sure would be cool to run around my desktop as Mario!"
No? Oh well then.
Introducing..
Windows 98 in Super Mario Maker!

This mod features four semisolids, new ground, new mushrooms, new hard & ice blocks, and bridges!
But you're thinking.. Buntendo, you silly B#$ch! You can only do three semisolids.
But I reply, But it's quite simple to make the fourth semisolid in this theme!
Just place the bridge over the top of any semisolid, and extend it to the length of the semisolid!
Then you have gray, blue, red, and green.
