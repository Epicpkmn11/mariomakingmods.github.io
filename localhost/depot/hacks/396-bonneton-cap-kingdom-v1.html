Originally posted to Gamebanana, three months ago. This was my first public theme, so don't expect something too high quality.
Expect a remake soon, maybe, possibly.

Super Mario Odyssey's Cap Kingdom is spooky. So why not bring it into Super Mario Maker..?




This mod replaces SMB1's boring Haunted House theme, with a more upbeat, but spooky theme based on Bonneton! If the tileset wasn't enough, you get to listen to some sweet 8-BIT Music by the incredibly talented Tater-Tot Tunes!

You can expect some more mods from me later on, as it seems like Mario Maker's Wii U modding scene is a bit slow.

How to install: Drag the contents of the downloaded file into your content folder! It's that simple.
