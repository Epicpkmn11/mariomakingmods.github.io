Hello everyone, here is a big update on the Piranha Island mod that I posted a while ago.
It is now a full mod and replaces almost everything in SMW !

This mod is entirely based on the Super Mario World Piranha Island romhack.





In this picture the ground under the tanks is actually part of the background, just so you know.



The following screenshots are a bit outdated, as you play as Luigi in this mod and not Mario.




It replaces all themes with a general spooky ambience. I was unable to rip the musics from the romhack so I put soundtracks from other games instead.

Features:
-6 new tilesets
-6 new backgrounds
-new sprites
-invisible wings (for a better effect)
-new musics

Download Wii U

Credits to:
-PokerFace for most of the graphics
