It's the only way I found that wouldn't crash the game. So what is the simpler way to do it ? Because I don't see it in your guide.
Posted by Lakifume

Extract your SZS with SARCExtract, open the Output.bfres in BFRES Tool, it'll export dds files from the bfres, so then you open them in paint.net or gimp 2 and edit them, then save them as A8R8G8B8 DDS files, then in BFRES tool, click the button for the sprite you want to replace, locate your DDS file, and it'll automatically write it to the Output.bfres. Then you can use SARCPacker or even Wexos's Toolbox to make a new SARC, then use libyaz0 to compress it to a szs file, then open the original szs and the new szs in a hex editor, and if there's a 20 at offset 0 byte 0A in the original, put it there in the new one. The hex editing for byte 0A is not necessary for M1-MW tilesets.
