 		This tutorial uses SD Cafiine, in which all the files are hosted on your SD card. The advantage to using SD Cafiine is that you can do this tutorial if you are offline (you need Haxchi for this) and you don't need a PC. The disadvantage is that if you are a beta tester or you are a theme developer, every time you want to change your files, you'll have to close your wii u, take out SD, edit, reinsert, launch hax, and etc...

If you want to have everything hosted on your PC, I'd advise you follow our Cafiine tutorial.

Keep in mind that you can't do this with CBHC (Coldboot Haxchi). You'll have to uninstall it first.

What you need

- SD Cafiine

1. Put the extracted SDCafiine.zip contents onto the root of your SD Card.

2. Make a new folder. You'll need to name it with the Title ID for the game that you are hacking. Here is a list of all the title IDs for your specific game.

3. Insert the mods content into the newly created folder.

4. Go to the Homebrew Laucher and load SD Caffine.
- It will take you to the home screen

5. Now launch the game that you wanted to mod.

You have just launched your modified game! Enjoy!
